Why Choose a Long Distance VTC Over Train or Plane?

The Thalys to Paris, the Eurostar to London, low-cost flights — there are plenty of options. But when you look more closely:

  • Door to door — No trip to the station or airport. No connections. Depart from your address, arrive at the destination address.
  • No schedule constraints — Leave when you want. No ticket to exchange, no delayed or cancelled train.
  • Productivity — Onboard WiFi, comfortable seats, silence. Work, make calls or rest. Impossible in a crowded train.
  • Cost-effective for 2-3 people — A VTC for 3 often costs less than 3 Thalys or plane tickets + transfers.
  • Unlimited luggage — No supplement, no weight limit. Suitcases, equipment, professional gear — everything fits.
