Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Controller

Belvia Drive, passenger transport company, registered in Brussels-Capital Region, Belgium.
Email: contact@belviadrive.be | Phone: +32 496 20 11 12

2. Data Collected

  • Identification data: name, first name, email address, phone number
  • Booking data: pickup address, destination, date and time, number of passengers, vehicle type
  • Browsing data: pages visited, visit duration, browser type (via anonymised analytics cookies)
  • Payment data: no banking data is stored on our servers. Transactions are processed by PCI-DSS certified payment providers.

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Execution and tracking of transport bookings
  • Communication related to your rides (confirmation, reminder, changes)
  • Invoicing
  • Service improvement
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Legal Basis

  • Execution of the transport contract (GDPR Article 6.1.b)
  • Consent for marketing communications (Article 6.1.a)
  • Legitimate interest for service improvement (Article 6.1.f)
  • Legal obligation for invoicing (Article 6.1.c)

5. Data Retention

  • Booking data: 2 years after last service
  • Billing data: 7 years (Belgian legal requirement)
  • Browsing data: 13 months (analytics cookies)
  • Marketing data: until consent withdrawal

6. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold. It may be shared with:

  • The assigned driver (name, address, schedule)
  • Payment providers (transactional data only)
  • Competent authorities if legally required

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction: limit processing of your data
  • Right to portability: receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object: object to processing for legitimate reasons

To exercise these rights: contact@belviadrive.be

8. Cookies

Our site uses strictly necessary cookies and anonymised analytics cookies (no advertising cookies). You can manage your preferences via your browser settings.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, modification, disclosure or destruction.
